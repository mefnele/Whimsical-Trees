The thought behind the Whimsical Trees

I have always been deeply interested in design, details, and creativity. In my leisure time, I use my phone and a pen to create drawings in PenUp, a pre-installed app by Samsung. It’s an awesome program that allows me to bring my reveries to life through illustration.

When working on my drawings, I dedicate countless hours to refining and perfecting even the smallest details—zooming in to the very last pixels. This adds intricacies that are only noticeable when viewed up close. My drawings reflect my fascination with escapism.

I immerse myself in fantasy-like scenarios, where skies radiate with vibrant hues, trees twist and contort, and objects levitate, converging into the abyss. The result is surrealistic imagery—where abstraction, fantasy, and reality blend into a single whimsical composition. That is the essence of my drawings.

I have then decided to program this website using HTML, as it serves as the foundation of web development. I chose it because of the vast online community, with tutorials and forums that guided me whenever I got stuck. I customized the CSS to match the website’s theme, basing it on my preferred color schemes and text fonts. I've enjoyed learning how to apply code, much like I enjoy refining the details of my drawings. To me, both aspects go hand in hand. Because both of them require meticulousness.

That’s why I decided to create this website—it serves as a visual representation my interest in digital design.

As I continue to refine my skills, I look forward to expanding my gallery of whimsical drawings.
