The thought behind the Whimsical Trees

My drawings reflect my fascination with escapism and warped realities. I have always enjoyed drawing, designing, and creating. In my leisure time, I use my phone and a stylus pen to create drawings in PENUP, a pre-installed painting app created by Samsung. It’s an awesome program that allows me to bring my imaginative worlds to life through illustration.

When working on my drawings, I dedicate countless hours to refine even the smallest details—zooming in to the last pixels. This adds details that are only noticeable when viewed up close. I appreciate it when a composition consists of various details. It brings depth to it.

When I draw, I immerse myself in these surrealistic scenarios, where skies radiate with vibrant hues, trees twist and contort, and objects levitate, converging into the abyss. So I draw them. The result is fantasy-like imagery — where abstraction, fantasy, and reality blend into a whimsical composition. That is sort of the whole gimmick of the drawings. It is great that I can utilize my phone to illustrate my reveries. And then I needed a platform to present my drawings.

So I decided to program this website using HTML. I chose to use HTML because it serves as the foundation of web development. I also chose it because of the vast online community, with tutorials and forums that guided me whenever I got stuck. I customized it according to my preferred color schemes and text fonts. I've enjoyed learning how to apply code, much like I enjoy refining the details of my drawings. Both aspects go hand in hand, as both require patience and meticulousness.

It is pretty fun to create this gallery, and my programming abilities keep growing as I attempt to make alterations.

As I refine my skills, I look forward to expanding my gallery of surrealistic drawings.

Thanks for reading! :D
